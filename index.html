Note Your Tasks

Busy days are just the norm of modern life, so how do keep up with the endless stream of chores and events flooding our day to day? We write them down in the app.

Rank Them Based On Importance

"So you created another note taking App?"
No, not quite. Essentialism lets you rank each entry based on importance. As you enter more and more tasks, the important ones float up while the small sink to a separate pile, better suited for when all the huge tasks are take care of.

Focus

Once the important tasks are prioritized you are able to focus and plow down the things that weigh you down the most, so you can live your most essential life.
